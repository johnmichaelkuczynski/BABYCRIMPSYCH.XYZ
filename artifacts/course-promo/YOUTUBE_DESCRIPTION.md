## Description

Basic Criminal Psychology is a complete, self-paced course that takes you through a full unit of criminal psychology — from what the field actually is to how experts predict dangerousness — and teaches, tutors, drills, and grades the whole thing with AI.

This is a substantial course, not a summary. One full unit, eight topics, each with its own lessons, practice, and graded assessment:

1.1 What criminal psychology is
1.2 Why people offend
1.3 Inside the psychopath
1.4 Profiling the offender
1.5 Eyewitnesses and memory
1.6 Interrogation and false confessions
1.7 Madness and the law
1.8 Predicting danger

What you get inside the course:

📖 Three-depth lessons — read any topic at Short, Medium, or Long length, rewritten to your chosen depth while preserving the same ideas, examples, and learning objectives.
✨ Examples on demand — expand any lesson into a version with worked, real-to-life illustrations for every point.
💬 A section-scoped AI tutor — ask about the exact passage you're reading and get a grounded answer streamed back in real time.
🎯 Adaptive practice — generated problem sets that get harder as you build a streak and ease off after a miss, with an explanation on every question.
✅ AI-graded coursework — two homework sets, a timed unit test, and a cumulative final, each scored against model answers with written, per-question feedback.
🛡️ Built-in academic integrity — every submission is screened by two independent layers: a text classifier (GPTZero) and a keystroke-pattern detector that catches AI use even when the words are reworded.
🧭 Diagnostic reasoning assessments — two instruments (criminal-psychology subject reasoning and general reasoning), offered in multiple formats and lengths at four points across the course; ungraded, takeable anytime, never affecting your grade.
📊 Live analytics — track attempts, accuracy, streaks, and per-topic mastery as you go.

Built for adults approaching the discipline for the first time — including graduate students and academics moving into a new field.
